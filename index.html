My Services

Computer Vision & AI Projects

I specialize in Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence, leveraging my expertise in Python, OpenCV, Tensorflow, and YOLO. My portfolio showcases impactful projects like EnVision AI, Parirakshak, and the Rescue Drone. These ventures involved real-time anomaly detection, violence detection in CCTV networks, and the development of a rescue drone prototype. Through these initiatives, I integrated cutting-edge technologies to solve critical problems, utilizing tools like Docker, Machine Learning, and specific hardware like NVIDIA servers and Raspberry Pi. My work in Computer Vision extends to practical applications, enhancing surveillance systems and contributing to innovative solutions for emergency situations.

Embedded Systems Expertise

My proficiency in Embedded Systems encompasses a spectrum of skills, including Embedded C, VHDL, and hands-on experience with hardware like Jeston NX Xavier, Jetson Nano, Raspberry Pi. I actively participated in hackathons such as e-Yantra Hardware Hackathon 2023, where our team created a prototype rescue drone. Utilizing Python, OpenCV, YOLO, and MobileNetSSD, we developed a real-time human detection mechanism crucial for emergency scenarios. Through my knowledge of hardware interfacing and software integration, I contribute to innovative solutions, bridging the gap between software and hardware domains to address real-world challenges.
